BWA Video — Final Story Structure
From the Worst Leader in the Navy to 2 Startup Acquisitions - Lead from the deck plates, not from the office
One Line Topic
A founder who learned that leadership means being in the room — not above it.
Target Audience
Early-stage founders and aspiring entrepreneurs who think leadership means having the best vision, working the hardest, or being the smartest — and need to hear it's actually about being present with your team.
What They Get
A real story (not advice) that shifts how they think about leading teams, told by someone honest enough to admit he got it wrong first.
